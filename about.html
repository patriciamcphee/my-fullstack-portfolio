How I became a web developer

How it started: I've been in IT in different capacities for the past 25 years, starting in support and moving to network system administration. However, for the past 20 years, I've been working as a Technical Writer. My expertise is taking the guesswork out of complex technical content for busy IT professionals.

How I got here: About 12 years ago, I started to become disenchanted with my career choice. I explored different careers like voiceover, going back to my roots as a Graphic Designer. I even considered transitioning to UX/UI Design, Instructional Design, or Web Development. After exploring each of these possibilities, I decided to become a Full Stack Web Developer.

UX/UI Design and Instructional Design seem like easy transitions for a Technical Writer aiming to change careers, but for me, I wanted something challenging. Plus, from observation, everyone and their dog are transitioning to these two careers. And for me, these two careers wouldn't be much of a challenge.
