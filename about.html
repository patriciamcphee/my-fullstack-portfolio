How I became a web developer

How it started: I've been in IT in different capacities for the past 25 years, starting in support and moving to network system administration. However, for the past 20 years, I've been working as a Technical Writer. My expertise is taking the guesswork out of complex technical content for busy IT professionals.

How I got here: About 12 years ago, I became disenchanted with my career choice. So I started to explore different careers like Medical Writer, Voiceover/Voice Actor, or returning to my roots as a Graphic Designer. I even considered transitioning to UX/UI Designer, Instructional Designer, or Web Developer. After exploring these possibilities, I decided on Full Stack Web Developer.

Yes, UX/UI Design and Instructional Design seem like an easy transition for a Technical Writer aiming to change careers, but I wanted something challenging. Plus, from observation, everyone and their dog are transitioning to these two careers. And for me, these two careers wouldn't be much of a challenge.
