About

How it started

I've been in IT in different capacities for the past 25 years, starting in support and moving to network system administration. However, for the past 20 years, I've been working as a User Experience (UX) Technical Writer taking the guesswork out of complex technical applications and corresponding content. Over the last 12 years, I've helped organizations reduce, simplify, and restructure their content, ensuring quality and accuracy, and increasing customer satisfaction rates.

How I got here

About 12 years ago, I became disenchanted with my career choice. So I started to explore different careers like Medical Writer, Voiceover/Voice Actor or returning to my roots as a Graphic Designer. I even considered transitioning to UX/UI Designer, Instructional Designer, or Web Developer.

After exploring these possibilities, I decided on Full Stack Web Developer. I'm currently enrolled in CareerFoundry's Full Stack Web Development program. My goal is to bring my programming skills up to par and graduate by September 2022. After that, I'll reach for and grab a UX Design certificate by March 2023 to transition into a UX/UI Design role.
